Chevrolet Vehicles Lug Patterns

Vehicle Years Covered Lug Pattern
Silverado 1500 1999-Present 6x139.7 mm (6x5.5")
Silverado 2500HD/3500HD 2001-Present 8x165.1 mm (pre-2011), 8x180 mm (2011+)
Tahoe 2000-Present 6x139.7 mm (6x5.5")
Suburban 2000-Present 6x139.7 mm (6x5.5")
Colorado 2004-2012, 2015-Present 6x120 mm
Trailblazer 2002-2009, 2021-Present 6x127 mm
Equinox 2005-Present 5x120 mm
Traverse 2009-Present 6x120 mm
Malibu 2000-Present 5x115 mm
Impala 2000-2020 5x115 mm
Camaro 2010-Present 5x120 mm
Blazer 2019-Present 5x120 mm

Chevrolet Fitment Notes

  • Silverado 1500 models share lug pattern with Tahoe and Suburban, allowing for direct wheel compatibility.
  • 2011+ Heavy Duty models use an 8x180mm pattern which is not backward compatible with older models.
  • Equinox and Camaro share the 5x120mm pattern but require different center bore sizes.
  • Colorado and Traverse both use 6-lug patterns but with different bolt circle diameters.
  • Most passenger cars (Malibu, Impala) use the GM standard 5x115mm pattern.
